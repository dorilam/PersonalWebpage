Phi Delta Alpha

Phi Delta Alpha is the first Asian-interest sorority created in the state of Oklahoma. It was officially founded on February 4, 2002 by 14 ambitious women. They dedicated their hearts and souls into creating an everlasting sisterhood, which would provide Asian-American women the chance to enter into the Greek system. 16 years later, the Alpha chapter of Phi Delta Alpha has successfully crossed 154 noble women into the sorority.

As a member of this organization, I've help countless roles in order to stand close to the pillars of the sorrority and seek ways to improve its functions. Some roles I've held include being Director of Social Affairs and New Memeber Education.
